About

DarkStrider

Darkstrider is a 2D beat ‘em up & hack and slash game, set in a fantasy world. The main character (Darkstrider), revived by a necromancer, embarks on a journey to destroy the dark magic that corrupted the kingdom of Woria. Games such as Castle Crashers, Hades, Hyper Light Drifter, Towertale, Dead Trigger were an inspiration for this game. The graphic style of the game is similar to hand-drawn movies, with frame-by-frame cartoon animations and illustrated cutscenes.
